Just hack it!

about

RealityHack is the hackathon that makes reality matter. It’s a groundbreaking series of exciting programming challenges organized by Estimote, both online and offline. The goal is to build stunning apps, that go beyond the boundaries of smartphones and reach into the physical world, creating magical experiences for fellow humans.

RealityHack @ TAURON Arena Kraków is going to change the way event attendees and concert goers experience the excitement of this amazing facility.

The Hackathon takes place at 9:00am on Saturday, June 11th, 2016. Estimote and TAURON Arena Kraków are calling for hackers and designers to come to the arena and unleash their creativity. Build apps enhancing the visitor experience to win fame and glory!

And don’t forget about the before party on June 10th, 7:00pm at the Old Chocolate factory on Krakusa 11, where we will discuss the structure and rules of the hackathon, answer all your questions, and provide helpful hints and templates to ensure we can help make your apps and dream become a reality.

organizers

TAURON Arena Krakow

TAURON Arena Kraków is the biggest indoor event space in Poland and one of the most modern in the world. It has a capacity for over 20,000 people and hosted world-class events from sports championships to shows by the Foo Fighters, Elton John, Hans Zimmer, and Katy Perry.

Estimote

Estimote creates an operating system for the physical world. The world’s leading brands, including nearly 70% of Fortune 100, use Estimote Beacons to build the next generation of location-aware applications in retail, hospitality, and culture. Estimote is backed by leading Silicon Valley investors and is a graduate of the famed Y Combinator program.

Details

We kick off at 9:00 AM sharp and your team will have eight hours to develop a mobile app in one of the following categories:

  • Indoor navigation
  • Show experience
  • Food & beverage
  • Merchandise
  • Prizes and awards
  • VIP experience

Indoor navigation
Use beacons to make it a breeze for people to find their way around the arena.

Show experience
Think how you can use location context so people can have more fun at concerts and matches. WIll it be finding friends, hyperlocal media streams, or even posting things to the giant overhead screen? Up to you!

Food & beverage
Beacons can help you reduce queues, or even make sure people have their food and beverage delivered to their seats.

Merchandise
Think about how a location-powered app can help tap this revenue stream with coupons, special offers, and tickets.

Prizes and awards
Fans want to connect with the performers and athletes they admire: give them a chance with seat upgrades, lotteries, and contextual games.

VIP experience
Imagine the ways in which a mobile app can ensure you get a touch of luxury, from calling for service to taking part in special auctions.

Mentors

There will be experienced mentors on hand to help advise and guide you, ensuring that you can bring the vision of your app to life.

We also have a before-party planned for Friday night, 7pm to 9pm, so we can prepare you with all the tools for success! We’ll chat about Use Cases, workshop some ideas, and of course, provide food and drinks.

Make sure you drop in at the Old Chocolate Factory, Krakusa 11, Friday June 10th at 7pm!

We’ll also make sure you’re well fed and hydrated, but you’ll need your own computer and an iOS/Android device to work on. Apart from that, good attitude is a must!

If you are interested in volunteering to mentor at the RealityHack, drop us a line at RealityHack@estimote.com

Prizes

Every team will be provided Estimote Beacons to complete their projects. While we will program in one of the TAURON Arena Kraków’s large ballrooms, your apps will be showcased for judging throughout the arena, demonstrating how they will enhance experience for event attendees.

The Winning team will receive a standing ovation, exciting prizes, and the bragging rights of having hacked the TAURON Arena Kraków!

Stay tuned for more information
